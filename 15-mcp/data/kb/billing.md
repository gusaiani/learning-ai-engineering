# Billing and invoices

NovaCRM bills monthly on the calendar day you first subscribed. Charges are
attempted up to three times over five days if the card is declined; after that
the account is downgraded to Free and Pro features are read-only.

Invoices are emailed within 24 hours of a successful charge and are also
available under **Settings → Billing → Invoices**. Tax is added at checkout
based on the billing address country. We do not issue paper invoices.

To change the card on file, go to **Settings → Billing → Payment method**.
The first charge on a new card is authorized for $1 and immediately voided —
this is normal and not an actual charge.

Annual plans are paid up front; pro-rated refunds for downgrades follow the
refund policy in `refunds.md`.
