# NovaCRM — Pricing & Plans

## Plans overview

NovaCRM offers three plans: Free, Pro, and Enterprise. All plans include the core CRM features (contacts, deals, pipelines). Higher tiers unlock automation, integrations, and team collaboration tools.

## Free plan

- **Price:** $0/month
- **Users:** 1
- **Contacts:** Up to 500
- **Deals:** Up to 100 active deals
- **Pipelines:** 1
- **Storage:** 100 MB file storage
- **Support:** Community forum only
- **API access:** No
- **Integrations:** None

The Free plan is intended for solo users evaluating NovaCRM. It does not include API access, integrations, or email automation. Upgrading to Pro unlocks all integrations and removes contact/deal limits.

## Pro plan

- **Price:** $49/month per user (billed monthly) or $39/month per user (billed annually)
- **Users:** Up to 25
- **Contacts:** Unlimited
- **Deals:** Unlimited
- **Pipelines:** Up to 10
- **Storage:** 10 GB file storage
- **Support:** Email support, 24-hour response time (business days)
- **API access:** Yes — 1,000 requests/minute
- **Integrations:** Slack, Gmail, Outlook, Zapier, HubSpot import
- **Automation:** Up to 50 workflow automations
- **Reports:** Standard reports + 10 custom report templates

The Pro plan is designed for small to mid-size sales teams. Most customers start here. The annual billing discount saves $120/user/year.

## Enterprise plan

- **Price:** $199/month per user (billed monthly) or $159/month per user (billed annually)
- **Users:** Unlimited
- **Contacts:** Unlimited
- **Deals:** Unlimited
- **Pipelines:** Unlimited
- **Storage:** 100 GB file storage
- **Support:** Priority support, 4-hour response time, dedicated account manager
- **API access:** Yes — 10,000 requests/minute
- **Integrations:** All Pro integrations + Salesforce migration, custom webhooks, SSO (SAML/OIDC)
- **Automation:** Unlimited workflow automations
- **Reports:** All reports + custom SQL queries + scheduled email reports
- **Security:** SSO, audit logs, role-based access control, IP allowlisting, SOC 2 compliance

Enterprise is for larger teams that need advanced security, compliance, and custom integrations. Contact sales for volume discounts on 100+ users.

## Billing FAQ

**Can I switch plans at any time?**
Yes. Upgrades take effect immediately and are prorated. Downgrades take effect at the end of the current billing period. You keep access to higher-tier features until then.

**What payment methods do you accept?**
Credit card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex) and ACH bank transfer (Enterprise only). We do not accept PayPal or cryptocurrency.

**Do you offer refunds?**
We offer a full refund within 14 days of your first payment on a new plan. After that, we do not issue refunds but you can cancel anytime to stop future charges. Annual plans are refunded on a prorated basis within the first 30 days.

**Is there a free trial for Pro?**
Yes. Every new account gets a 14-day Pro trial with full features. No credit card required. After the trial, you can subscribe or drop to the Free plan.

**Do you offer discounts for nonprofits or education?**
Yes. Verified nonprofits and educational institutions get 30% off any plan. Contact support@novacrm.com with proof of status.

**What happens if I exceed the API rate limit?**
Requests beyond the limit receive a 429 (Too Many Requests) response. The response includes a Retry-After header. No charges apply for rate-limited requests.
