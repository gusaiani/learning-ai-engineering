# Refund policy

Hardware orders (NovaCam, NovaMic, NovaLight) can be refunded within 30 days
of delivery if returned in original condition. Customers initiate a refund
from **Orders → [order] → Request refund**, which creates a refund_requested
ticket for support to review.

Software subscriptions (Pro, Team, Enterprise) are refundable within 14 days
of the most recent charge if no Pro features have been used during that
period. After 14 days, downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle and
no pro-rated refund is issued, with one exception: annual plans are
pro-rated to the nearest full unused month.

Shipping fees are non-refundable. Refunds land on the original payment
method within 5–10 business days. Customers are notified by email when the
refund is processed.

Edge cases (lost in transit, damaged on arrival, billing error on our side)
bypass the 30-day window — escalate to a senior agent.
